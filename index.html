About

With years of dedicated expertise in the world of luxury watches and fine jewelry, I offer unmatched service in the auction and valuation of prestigious timepieces and exquisite jewelry items.

I specialize in renowned luxury brands including Rolex, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet, Cartier, Van Cleef & Arpels, and Bulgari, with a profound knowledge of both vintage and contemporary pieces.

My commitment to transparency, integrity, and precision ensures that every client receives the highest level of service, whether you're seeking expert valuation, consigning a treasured piece, or acquiring a new addition to your collection.

Areas of Expertise

  • Vintage and contemporary luxury watches
  • Rolex, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet
  • Fine jewelry from Cartier, Van Cleef & Arpels, Bulgari
  • Estate and antique pieces
  • Diamond and colored gemstone jewelry
  • Natural pearls and rare timepieces

Market Insights

Watch Market Trends

Growing demand for vintage watches and dress watches over sports models, with particular interest in rare complications and limited editions.

Jewelry Market

Strong market for quality pieces with provenance, especially signed pieces from renowned houses with historical significance or exceptional craftsmanship.
